Australia's Biodiversity Crisis

The Race to Protect Our Unique Wildlife through the Collaborative Australian Protected Areas Database (CAPAD)

The Grand Sanctuary

Australia is one of the world's most "megadiverse" nations. Our vast landscapes and surrounding oceans are home to species found nowhere else on Earth.

The CAPAD framework maps out the sanctuaries where nature is given a fighting chance. From the rugged Outback to the vibrant Great Barrier Reef, these are the lines we've drawn in the sand to protect life.
